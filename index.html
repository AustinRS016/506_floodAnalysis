This map shows the flood risk( fema ) in the city of Everett in relation to public schools, inter-city transit, major roads, potential refuge centers and a social vulnerability index.

The flood risk is calculated by FEMA (Federal Emergency Management Agency) and is displayed in 3 categories:

  • Depicted in red are areas in the base flood plane.
  • Depicted in orange are areas with a 1% annual chance of flooding/26% chance of flooding during a 30 year mortgage.
  • Depicted in yellow are areas with a 0.2% annual chance of flooding.

Social vulnerability index ( svi ) ranks each cenus tract based on 14 social factors and is used to determine which communites are most likely in need of support surrounding a hazardous event. Census tracts are assinged ranks on a scale from 0 (lowest) to 1 (highest). Click on a tract to reveal its SVI score. Read more here.
